Automation Fan by Calvin

Introduction

This is an automation fan that uses the microbit to allow the fan to operate, such as turning off or on. The materials required for this project are:

  • DC Motor
  • Microbit
  • Wires
  • LEDs
  • Two 9V Batteries
  • Microbit Battery
  • Resistors
  • Transistor

What is the purpose?

Problem it solves. Why is it important?

This prototype can solve many problems, such as laziness in citizens. It allows you to leave children at home during hot summers or cold winters. The purpose of this device is to cool or heat the room according to the temperature without having to touch anything.

Who will use this device? How will it benefit them?

The elderly, children, babies, teenagers, young adults, and adults.

What are the main features of your prototype? How do they address the identified need?

The main feature of this prototype is the cooling system. It allows those who are disabled or lazy to enjoy air conditioning without lifting a finger.

How does it work?

When we press button A, the value increases by 1. Press button B to enter. After entering a 4-digit password, it will open and show a check mark if the password is correct, or a cross if it is wrong.
