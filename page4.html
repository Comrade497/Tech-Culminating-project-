Garage door

Production

Have you ever gone out of your car to open your garage door thinking how inconvenient that is? Have you ever wondered if there was a solution to this problem? We at home automation Incorporated Have created the remote controlled garage door. The remote is paired seamlessly with your pre-existing garage door through the use of radio signals delivered between two computer chips. when the on button is pressed on the remote the door will open when the off button is pressed by the garage door it will close and vice versa. Demo video below micro bits are used, the A button being the on switch and B being off.
