Automation Door from Manson Lo

introdution

It is an automation door that using a password to open. It material is:

  • a big card board
  • a microbit
  • some wire
  • a servo motor
  • two 3A battery
  • battery for microbit

purpose

Problem it solve. Why is it important?

This prototype can resolve people who always forgot their keys and lock themselves outside , at last need to waste a lots of time and money . Second , it is much more save than a traditional lock , cause it can't be break with a few paper clip .

Target consumers

Forgetful people e.g. elderly , child , some people who are just forgetful , people live their own , people live in houses .

Main features of my prototype

safe and convenient

How is it work?

When we press button A it value increase 1, press button B to enter. After we enter a 4 digit password, it'll open and show a check mark if it is right, won't open and show a cross if it's wrong.
